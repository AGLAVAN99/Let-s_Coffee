Concept

The idea brings together the online and offline part of having a coffee with either a friend or a new person. We know how hard socializing is, so we do the hard work for you.

What do we do?

We match you with a random person that shares the same passions as you do and then we deliver it to your home and as a bonus, we organize an online meeting. If you are more of an on-site person, don't worry, we have multiple thematic locations that you will surely enjoy.

Why us?

Driven by a young team, we bring the socializing concept to a next level and provide high quality coffee-based products. If you are not convinced, you can stop by and have a cup!
